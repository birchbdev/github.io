About Me

I'm Bryan, a software engineer with a background in ICU and interventional radiology nursing. My journey from bedside care to building clinical software and embedded systems was driven by a desire to make healthcare tools smarter, more efficient, and more human-centered.

After earning my computer science degree, I began designing systems that bridge the physical and digital — from microcontroller-based tools like RFID-enabled scuba gear trackers and ESP32 weather stations, to full-stack apps that streamline hospital procedure scheduling or provide real-time CPR feedback.

Whether I’m designing a cross-platform app with Vue and Capacitor, integrating embedded hardware, or applying AI to support staff workflows, my focus is always on clarity, usability, and impact.

Featured Projects

💊 Smart Medication Dispenser
